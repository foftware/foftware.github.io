Foftware - a functional software.

We are a group of programmers who believe in the benefits of functional software and embrace functional design principles. We love to take care of all aspects of software during its lifetime. This includes

  • gathering and clarifying requirements,
  • design and architecture,
  • implementing both frontend and backend,
  • building the infrastructure,
  • operations and maintenance.

We enjoy learning about various problem domains and strive for clean and simple design and implementation.

We organize and maintain FPBrno meetup in our spare time.

Let us know if you have an interesting problem for us to solve. Our language of choice is Haskell with years of professional experience, but we are open to other, preferably functional, stacks as well.
